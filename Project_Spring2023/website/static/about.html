we are bizou
              At Notary Singh, our story began as a collaborative effort between four dedicated Baruch College students, each bringing their unique skills and expertise to the table. It all started when one of our team members, a licensed notary public, recognized the need for a more accessible and convenient notarization solution. With a shared passion for innovation and a drive to make a difference, we embarked on a mission to revolutionize the notary industry.Our diverse backgrounds have been instrumental in shaping the services we provide today.
            Our team members with Masters in Information Systems, specializing in Data Analytics, have helped optimize our platform for a seamless user experience, while our Cybersecurity expert has ensured the highest level of security and data protection. By combining our individual strengths, we've created a versatile and reliable E-Notary and mobile notary service that caters to the ever-evolving needs of our clients. We take pride in our journey, and we're committed to continually refining and expanding our services, making notarization more accessible and secure for everyone.

our values

human first

At Notary Singh, we're passionate about putting people first. We understand that the notarization process can be complex and confusing, which is why we're dedicated to making it as easy and stress-free as possible. Our experienced team is here to provide personalized support and guidance, so that you can rest assured that your notarial needs are in good hands.

creativity & fun

We believe that the notarization process doesn't have to be dull or tedious - in fact, it can be downright enjoyable. Our team of creative professionals is dedicated to finding new and exciting ways to inject a little bit of fun and personality into every interaction.

no ego

partners, not clients

We believe that every notarial need represents a unique opportunity to build a lasting relationship based on trust, respect, and mutual benefit. That's why we strive to understand your specific needs and goals, so that we can provide personalized solutions that meet and exceed your expectations. Whether you're an individual, a small business owner, or a large corporation, we're committed to working with you as a partner to help you achieve your goals.
